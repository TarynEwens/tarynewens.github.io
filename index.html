About me

Fun Fact: I taught myself to write HTML when I was about 10 years old, so I could build websites about my virtual "dogz"!

It wasn't until I'd spent a number of years working in office administration, and then another few years as a pastry chef making handmade confectionery, that I decided to pull my passion for creating websites from side hobby status to the forefront.

After hanging up my apron, I joined General Assembly's full time Web Development Immersive bootcamp, and quickly discovered that learning to code was actually similar to learning a recipe. If you combine the right "ingredients" in the correct way, success! Otherwise, you might end up with a cake wreck on your hands.

Inspired by beautiful and quirky designs, I am seeking opportunities in Sydney, within a highly creative team who value openness and collaboration.


If you haven't already, you will quickly discover that fluffy dogs are my all-time favourite thing and yes, I would very much like to show you photos of my furbaby.
